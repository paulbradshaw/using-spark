Examples of using the Spark font for creating charts in text

There were 70 Airprox reports involving drones coming close to aircraft over the UK in 2016 - compared to 29 in 2015 and just 6 in 2014. There were no incidents at all between 2011 and 2013: 2010 {4,0,0,0,6,29,70} 2016. There have been 33 incidents up to May 2017.

That chart above is created using the font Spark: each bar of the chart is actually a number.

The font to turn it into a chart needs to be stored in the same place as the CSS file.

Using other chart fonts

Here's the same sentence again but using a different font (and class attribute) to create a different chart. Notice that it doesn't work properly because the numbers aren't kept within the range 0-9 (the numbers are {4,0,0,0,6,29,70})

There were 70 Airprox reports involving drones coming close to aircraft over the UK in 2016 - compared to 29 in 2015 and just 6 in 2014. There were no incidents at all between 2011 and 2013: 2010 {4,0,0,0,6,29,70} 2016. There have been 33 incidents up to May 2017.

Here it is again but with the numbers changed to {1,0,0,0,1,3,7}:

There were 70 Airprox reports involving drones coming close to aircraft over the UK in 2016 - compared to 29 in 2015 and just 6 in 2014. There were no incidents at all between 2011 and 2013: 2010 {1,0,0,0,1,3,7} 2016. There have been 33 incidents up to May 2017.
